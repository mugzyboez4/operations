Budget & JD Alignment
The CL job description has several mismatches with what was decided in the April 3 meeting. Not blocking lane definition, but needs resolution before JDs are final.

Aud Dev Integration — JD overstates CL authority. Meeting established Aud Dev operates independently.
OOH Media — in the JD, never discussed, not on the board.
Budget language — JD says Alignment and Distribution. Meeting decided Oversight & Strategy.
Digital as a department — JD still lists it separately. Restructuring absorbed it into Audience Dev.
Per-department spend accountability — deferred, no owner assigned.
Audience Data & Strategic Analytics
KPIs should come from Strategic Analytics. Audience Dev implements them. The data flow between the two isn't working — no systematic routing exists. Needs a defined handoff: who sends what, in what format, on what cadence.
Physical Packaging & D2C Production
No defined handoff from Creative to Commerce/D2C for physical packaging. Someone is filling the gap but it's not in anyone's lane. Needs visibility into who owns the cost-out process, the production timeline, and the handoff from creative direction to manufacturing.
Support Team Structure
Serves CL, Aud Dev, and Ops. Separate resources for Commercial. Organized by task, not roster (except asset requests). Specialized roles: Invoicing · Link generation · Scheduling (1 person + backup each).
