03
How Work Comes In

Creative is triggered by the Campaign Lead. Nothing starts without a ticket and a brief.

A Campaign Lead owns the campaign — strategy, budget, timeline — and opens a ticket with the brief. That ticket lands with the Creative Manager (production work) or Tickets & Routing (design requests), who confirms deliverables, budget envelope and timeline before any spend. From there it moves through the lanes below.
04

05
The Two Paths

Most work follows the same spine. The music-video and art/press paths differ at only a few points.

Music Video
Director treatments → creative call · production plan + call sheet · rough & final edit reviews · QC + delivery specs. Adds a shoot and a dedicated Video QC step.
Art & Press
Photographer + moodboards · art specs + press guidelines · selects + photo approvals. No shoot-QC lane — the review loop is photo selects and approvals.
