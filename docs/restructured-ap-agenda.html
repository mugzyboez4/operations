Active Projects Meeting — Restructured

WEDNESDAY AP AGENDA

45 minutes. Decision meeting — not a status update. Phase-tagged. Gate-aligned.
How this works: CL submissions come in via Teams message before the meeting (PLAN/ANNOUNCE/EXECUTE/DELIVER phase tag + what needs discussion). The meeting opens with DELIVER artists (release this week/next), then works backward through EXECUTE and ANNOUNCE. PLAN artists are async unless escalated.
Section 1: DELIVER DELIVER 15 MIN
Format
Artists releasing this week or next. What's live, what's blocked, what needs a decision right now.
Gate 4: CAN WE DELIVER? — All DSP deliveries confirmed, playlist pitches acknowledged, social calendar locked, press embargoes set.
Who speaks
Campaign Lead for each DELIVER artist. 2-3 minutes max per artist.
Output
Decisions captured. Blockers escalated. Action items assigned with owners.
Section 2: ANNOUNCE / EXECUTE EXECUTE ANNOUNCE 20 MIN
Format
Artists in active campaign or pre-announcement. Only items that need the room — not status updates.
Gate 2: CAN WE ANNOUNCE? / Gate 3: CAN WE EXECUTE? — Budget approved, creative set, departments briefed, masters delivered, DSP pitches submitted.
Who speaks
CLs only speak if they flagged something in their pre-submission. No round-robin.
Output
Cross-department dependencies surfaced. Blocked items identified. Next steps confirmed.
Section 3: PLAN PLAN 10 MIN
Format
New artists entering cycle. Scheduling requests. Only if escalated from async.
Gate 1: CAN WE PLAN? — A&R confirmed, physical projection returned, spend data pulled, audience profile delivered.
Default
PLAN artists are handled async via Monday Foundation Room unless a CL escalates to the room.
Meeting Rules
CL submissions due by 10am ET Wednesday via Teams message
Format: Artist name · Phase tag · What needs discussion or decision
Recap distributed same day — decisions, action items, carry-forwards
No status updates in the room. If it doesn't need a decision, it doesn't need a meeting.
