Campaign Operations / Campaign Leads

Campaign Ops is the translation layer. Campaign Leads is the execution layer. Campaign Ops ensures every department has the information it needs, when it needs it, in a format it can act on. Campaign Leads takes that information and builds the plan, owns the artist relationship, and drives the work to market. Neither function replaces the other. Both are incomplete alone.

Working draft — this framework evolves as the team builds. Your input shapes it.

When a decision gets made in one room, every other team knows what it means for them — and nothing falls through the cracks. That’s Campaign Ops. The team that turns that knowledge into action on the artist’s behalf? That’s Campaign Leads.
