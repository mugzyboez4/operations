Lane Definition

WHO DOES WHAT

RCA Records · Campaign Operations · April 2026
Campaign Leads
Campaign Strategy & Narrative
Defining how the story & narrative of the campaign go to market.
Timeline & Milestones
Create and maintain the campaign timeline.
Artist & Management Relations
Primary point of contact throughout the campaign lifecycle. Secure buy-in on direction and key decisions.
Budget & Allocation
Build the budget, manage allocation across departments, own spend decisions.
Trigger Key Asset Creation
Kick off the asset creation process — ads, announcements, highlight reels, UGC mashups.
Website Oversight
Trigger and oversee process of website creation and campaign tie-ins.
Out of Home & Trades Marketing
Oversee IRL media/trade media bookings and messaging.
Award Show Alignment
Oversee asset creation, advertising spends & placements and communication for Award Shows.
Pitch Deck Oversight
Responsible for the flow of Artist/Project Pitch Decks and department copy/content delivery timelines.
Physical Product Management
Oversee the configuration details and departmental follow-through for physical releases.
Artist Image Alignment
Oversee the process of image selects across Press, Partnerships and Packaging.
Promo Item Oversight
Ideate and oversee product content, budgeting, execution and distribution.
Audience Dev
Audience Definition & Research
Research and define target audience — demos, engagement rate, listening behavior and psychographics. In partnership with Strategic Analytics.
New Signing Audience Profiles
Baseline audience profile for newly signed artists entering the campaign cycle.
Performance & Reporting
Campaign reporting, KPIs, audience metrics. Present performance to leadership.
Audience Growth
Builds the strategy for audience growth and engagement in line with Creative's output and the overall campaign strategy.
Paid Media Campaign Execution
Online advertising campaigns across social channels, search and programmatic, leveraging internal data tools (FanSifter, etc.) to optimize performance.
Shortform Strategy & Execution
Taking artist shortform execution to the extent they will allow. Includes ideation, 3rd party booking and maintenance, first-person shoots and coaching.
Influencer Marketing
Responsible for all aspects of influencer campaigns from ideation through execution.
Seeding
Responsible for all paid narrative and discovery campaigns across social platforms.
UGC & Clipping
Ideation and execution of all UGC & clipping campaigns. Collaborates with CL, Creative and Publicity to identify key moments.
Engagement Campaigns
Building web-based campaigns (library scans, contests, etc.) to collect fan data and engage core audiences.
RCA Web & Social Property Management
Responsible for the flow and content across RCA's websites, newsletter and social properties.
IRL Event Collaboration
Collaborate with Creative to ensure the fan component of IRL events is prioritized to deliver reach and impact online during and/or following the event.
DSP & Partner Tools
Manage DSP marketing tools and partner marketing features.
Campaign Ops
Release Readiness
Monitor whether all departments, assets, and runway fields are on track for upcoming releases.
Cross-Department Visibility
Ensure every team has the context they need — artist status, budget gaps, dependencies, and blockers.
Action Items & Approvals
Follow up on action items to completion. Monitor pending management approvals.
Meeting Operations
Prep, capture, and distribute decisions and action items from all meetings.
Dashboards & System of Record
Build and maintain operational dashboards and the data infrastructure.
Release Scheduling
Check landscape and prerequisites, submit scheduling requests for executive review. Communicate approvals and denials back to Campaign Leads.
Support
Asset Routing
Submit creative tickets at the direction of Campaign Lead and Community Manager. Drive follow-through, prioritization, QA and approvals/routing.
Pre-Save Setup
Create and manage pre-saves at the Campaign Lead's direction. Includes SMF, third-party platforms (CoBrand, Laylo, etc.), and delivery of signup code for custom development.
Content Claims
Submit content claims across social platforms at the direction of the Campaign Lead and Community Manager.
Allow-Listing
Execute allow-listing requests for content teasing and social sharing for artist, fan, brands/licensees and friends/family accounts.
Takedowns
Submit takedown requests across social platforms. Source links as needed.
Profile Verification
Submit artist profile verification requests across socials.
Artist Newsletter Builds
Build, QA and send artist newsletters via FanSifter at the direction of the Community Manager.
Shortlink Generation
Manage all shortlink generation, pixel integration & QA across the roster.
Website Support
Submit creative requests, manage WebOps process and oversee pixel management. Drive follow-through, prioritization, QA and approvals/routing.
Vendors & Invoices
Oversee vendor and invoicing process inclusive of vendor setup, maintenance and invoice trafficking/processing across Campaign Strategy & Audience Development. Police the process with Campaign Operations.
Gratis Orders
Submit gratis orders at the direction of the Campaign Lead.
Team/Project Meeting Setup
Schedule and maintain multi-department meetings centered around artists/projects. Does not extend to huddles, regroups, etc.
Additional departments — Data & Analytics, Commercial Partnerships, Creative, and other departments will be mapped separately and added to this document as those conversations are completed.
