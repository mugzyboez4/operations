Campaign Leads
Campaign Strategy & Narrative
Define the story, the vision, and how the artist goes to market.
Artist & Management Relationship
Primary point of contact throughout the campaign lifecycle. Secure buy-in on direction and key decisions.
Creative Direction
Translate campaign vision into briefs for the Creative team — art direction, video, press images.
Budget & Allocation
Build the budget, manage allocation across departments, own spend decisions.
Timeline & Milestones
Create and maintain the campaign timeline.
Trigger Asset Creation
Kick off the asset creation process — ads, announcements, highlight reels, UGC mashups.
Website Oversight
Own artist website creation, updates, and QA.
Paid Ad Strategy
Define paid media strategy and provide assets. Media buying through Gupta. Platform-native execution moves to Audience Dev.
Release Scheduling
Own the scheduling process — identify target dates, confirm prerequisites, submit for executive approval.
Audience Dev
Audience Definition & Research
Research and define target audience — demos, platform health, listening behavior. In partnership with Strategic Analytics.
New Signing Audience Profiles
Baseline audience profile for newly signed artists entering the campaign cycle.
Performance & Reporting
Campaign reporting, KPIs, audience metrics. Present performance to leadership.
Audience Growth Strategy
Design the plan for growing the artist's fanbase across platforms.
Content & Social
Short form, social content, approvals, and platform presence — bios, pins, imagery, optimization.
Paid & Influencer Execution
Execute platform-native campaigns — influencer partnerships, fan pages, paid placements, targeting, and optimization.
Activation & Seeding
Fan engagement, seeding to tastemakers and communities, UGC campaigns, and organic growth.
RCA Social Properties
Manage @RCARecords accounts across platforms.
DSP & Partner Tools
Manage DSP marketing tools and partner marketing features.
Campaign Ops
Release Readiness
Monitor whether all departments, assets, and runway fields are on track for upcoming releases.
Cross-Department Visibility
Ensure every team has the context they need — artist status, budget gaps, dependencies, and blockers.
Action Items & Approvals
Follow up on action items to completion. Monitor pending management approvals.
Meeting Operations
Prep, capture, and distribute decisions and action items from all meetings.
Dashboards & System of Record
Build and maintain operational dashboards and the data infrastructure.
Support
Creative Requests & Asset Management
Enter requests into Airtable, track delivery, route for approval. Build pre-save pages.
Web, Links & Profiles
Website builds, smart links, YouTube metadata, platform profiles, channel creation, verification.
Finance & Vendor Admin
Vendor setup, invoice processing, internal fund transfers in Console. Directed by Campaign Leads.
Content & Claims
Content claims, takedowns, whitelisting, and DSP asset grid distribution.
Communications
Artist and label newsletters, texts, and distribution list management.
Scheduling & Logistics
Department calendar, marketing material shipping, and gratis product orders.
