Lane Definition

WHO DOES WHAT

RCA Records · Campaign Operations · April 2026
Campaign Leads
Decide Campaign Strategy & Narrative
Define the story, the vision, and how the artist goes to market.
Artist & Management Relationship
Primary point of contact for artist and management throughout the campaign lifecycle.
Own Campaign Budget & Allocation
Build the budget, manage allocation across departments, own spend decisions.
Plan Campaign Timeline & Milestones
Create and maintain the campaign timeline. Track key dates and deliverable deadlines.
Official Asset Management
Ensure all official assets — ads, announcements, highlight reels, UGC mashups — are created and delivered.
Website Oversight
Own artist website creation, updates, and QA.
Paid Ad Strategy & Assets
Define paid ad strategy and provide creative assets. Execution moves to Audience Dev.
Artist & Management Buy-in
Secure alignment from artist and management on campaign direction and key decisions.
Brief Creative on Campaign Narrative
Translate campaign vision into briefs for the Creative team — art direction, video, press images.
Release Scheduling Initiation
Identify target release date and communicate to Campaign Ops for scheduling request submission.
Audience Dev
Define the Audience
Research and define target audience segments — demos, platform health, listening behavior.
Build Audience Growth Strategy
Design the plan for growing the artist's fanbase across platforms.
Paid Ad Execution
Run paid campaigns — targeting, optimization, platform-native delivery. Strategy and assets come from CL.
Build & Activate Short Form Content
Strategy and execution for short form video across TikTok, Reels, Shorts. Collaborate with Creative on shoots.
Build Social Content & Manage Approvals
Create social content and manage the approval process with artist and management.
Activate Fan Engagement
Drive fan interaction — comments, community building, fan moments.
Activate Influencer Campaigns
Source, brief, and manage influencer partnerships — agency or direct.
Activate Seeding Campaigns
Seed music to tastemakers, curators, and communities ahead of release.
Manage & Amplify Platform Presence
Manage artist social profiles — bios, pins, smart URLs, profile imagery.
Amplify Organic Engagement
Grow and sustain organic reach through content strategy and platform optimization.
RCA Social Properties Management
Manage @RCARecords social accounts across platforms.
UGC Campaigns
Strategy and execution for user-generated content campaigns.
Campaign Reporting
Report on campaign performance, KPIs, and audience metrics.
DSP Marketing Tools
Manage DSP marketing tools and partner marketing features.
Define Audience for New Signings
Baseline audience profile for newly signed artists entering the campaign cycle.
Brief Management on Audience Performance
Present audience performance and strategic recommendations to leadership.
Campaign Ops
Release Readiness
Monitor whether all departments are on track for an upcoming release.
Asset Readiness & Deadlines
Ensure all assets needed for release are accounted for and hitting deadlines.
Release Scheduling Requests
Receive scheduling requests from CLs, verify prerequisites, submit for exec review.
Route Video Deliverables
Route video assets from management and Creative to the right departments.
Action Item Follow-Through
Follow up on action items from meetings until they are resolved.
Management Approval Status
Monitor pending management approvals and prompt when overdue.
System of Record Maintenance
Maintain the operational data layer — Airtable, dashboards, tracking tools.
Surface Budget Gaps & Divergence
Identify gaps between approved budgets and actual spend. Surface, don't approve.
Surface Meeting Decisions & Actions
Capture, format, and distribute decisions and action items from all meetings.
Surface Artist Context Across Departments
Ensure every department has the context they need about an artist's campaign status.
Surface Data via Dashboards & Tools
Build and maintain dashboards that give teams visibility into operational data.
Flag Release Runway Status
Flag when Release Runway fields are unresolved as a release date approaches.
Flag Upstream Dependencies
Identify and flag when one department is blocked by another's deliverable.
Prompt Cross-Dept Alignment
Proactively prompt departments to coordinate when timelines or plans require it.
Confirm YouTube Content Clearance
Confirm YouTube content is cleared through Earworm before publishing.
Support
Vendor Setup, Maintenance & Invoice Processing
Set up vendors, maintain records, process invoices. Directed by Campaign Leads.
Asset Management
Enter creative requests into Airtable, track delivery, route for approval.
Artist Pre-save Setup
Build pre-save pages — Sony Music Fans, co-brands, Laylo, etc.
Site Requests, Web Ops & Pixel Management
Handle website build requests, development coordination, and tracking pixel management.
Short Link Creation, Update & Distribution
Create and maintain smart links for releases and campaigns.
Content Claims & Takedowns
Manage content claims, whitelisting, fingerprints, and erroneous claim resolution.
Artist & Label Newsletters and Texts
Build and send artist newsletters, label newsletters, and text campaigns.
Internal Fund Transfers
Execute budget shifts in Console as directed by Campaign Leads.
Build & Deliver DSP Asset Grid
Compile and distribute the weekly DSP asset grid — artwork, photos, pitch blurb, genre.
Deliver YouTube Metadata & Descriptions
Enter and maintain YouTube video descriptions, tags, and metadata.
Maintain Distribution Lists
Keep internal music distribution lists current and accurate.
Profile Maintenance & Channel Creation
Set up new artist profiles, update bios and images across social and DSP. Strategy from Aud Dev.
Verification Requests
Submit and follow up on platform verification requests.
Manage Department Calendar
Maintain project meetings and department calendar. Not personal CL calendars.
Ship Marketing Materials & Promo
Coordinate FedEx shipments for marketing tools, promo items, and fan shipments.
Process Gratis Product Orders
Process orders for complimentary product — vinyl, merch, promo copies.
Additional departments — Data & Analytics, Commercial Partnerships, Creative, and other departments will be mapped separately and added to this document as those conversations are completed.
