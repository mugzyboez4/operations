Campaign Operations · RCA Records

The operating system for RCA campaigns.

Most releases follow the same shape: calm runway early, then a three-week scramble before street date. Blockers surface late. Decisions get made in hallway conversations because the meeting where they should've happened didn't have the right inputs.

It's not a people problem — it's a visibility problem. Across thirteen departments and a full roster, there's no shared place to see what's actually stuck and what's actually moving in time to do anything about it.

Stage-Gate is the fix. No new meetings, no approval gates, no oversight layer — just one shared way to see where every campaign sits and what's holding it up.

The Framework

Stage-Gate

Every campaign moves through four phases. Between each phase is a gate — a checkpoint, asked as a question. You don't move until the answer is yes. The system says no, not us.
