The Framework

Stage-Gate

Every campaign moves through four phases. Between each phase is a gate — a checkpoint, asked as a question. You don't move until the answer is yes. The system says no, not us.
01
PLAN
→ PLAN
CAN WE PLAN?
Do we know enough to start?
Closes when
Deal signed, artist brief exists, pod assigned.
02
EXECUTE
PLAN → EXECUTE
CAN WE EXECUTE?
Are we ready to build the campaign?
Closes when
Release date locked, marketing plan approved, timeline built, budget approved.
03
ANNOUNCE
EXECUTE → ANNOUNCE
CAN WE ANNOUNCE?
Are we ready to go public?
Closes when
Workability >70%, assets live, pre-save active, press plan confirmed, lead single ready.
04
DELIVER
ANNOUNCE → DELIVER
CAN WE DELIVER?
Did we hit the targets?
Closes when
Release week complete, focus track performing, all marketing beats hit.
