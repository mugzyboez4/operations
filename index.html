How to browse the shelf

  • Boards are live and moving — OPS HQ, the D2C calendar, the creative intake. Check them, don't archive them.
  • Reference is the stuff you look up — the campaign index, the creator roster, the workflow map. Stable, not breaking news.
  • Demos are working prototypes built to show a concept, not run production yet.
  • Working is scratch space — not for sharing outside the team.
  • Every department named on a card is who to ask, never who's on the hook. The shelf is a gift, not an audit.
What TEMPO is

Sony built the pipes — RDX. Nobody at the label reads the water. TEMPO is the human translation layer. It tracks, surfaces, flags, prompts, confirms. It never approves, decides, oversees, or manages. A map of who to ask, not a map of who's responsible.
