Prep Package — Management Intake Request
Input → feeds Intake, Dossier, Briefing
Isaia Huron
Sent to management at signing · Responses populate the Intake Assessment
How This Works: At signing, management provides the information below. The answers populate the Intake Assessment, which feeds the Dossier that every department receives before onboarding. One request covers 28 fields across 11 departments.
What We Need From Management
1. Team & Contacts
B&L, A&R Admin, Publicity, all depts 5 Qs ▶
Manager
Attorney
Publicist (if external)
Publishing (company/contact)
Anyone else we should know about? (Producer, creative director, day-to-day contact, etc.)
2. Creative & Visual
Creative, Marketing 3 Qs ▶
Does the artist have existing visual collaborators (photographers, directors, designers)?
Any existing brand guidelines or aesthetic direction we should follow?
Approval process for creative assets? (Who signs off, turnaround time)
3. Press & Public
Publicity, Marketing, Brand Partnerships 3 Qs ▶
How comfortable is the artist with press? Any boundaries or topics to avoid?
Is there an existing EPK, bio, or press kit we should have?
Any sensitivities we should be aware of? (Personal, cultural, things the artist is protective of)
4. Digital & Social
Digital/DSP 3 Qs ▶
Who currently owns/manages social accounts? Will that transfer or stay with the team?
Current distributor for existing catalog? (For Content ID/profile transfer)
Are Spotify for Artists / YouTube for Artists profiles verified and accessible?
5. Rights & Clearances
Sync, A&R Admin, B&L 4 Qs ▶
Does the artist have any existing masters not included in this deal? (Previous releases, features)
Are there any samples on upcoming recordings that need clearance?
For sync/licensing: who approves use of masters in film, TV, ads, games? Any restrictions?
Any co-owners, furnishers, or other parties with rights to current recordings?
6. Partnerships & Brand
Brand Partnerships, Sync 3 Qs ▶
Any existing brand partnerships, sponsorships, or endorsement deals?
Any brand categories that are off-limits? (Alcohol, tobacco, political, etc.)
Who approves use of the artist's name, likeness, and image for partnerships?
21 questions→ 28 intake fields→ 11 departments
9 fields this doesn't cover (RCA-internal): Radio lead & strategy · International team lead & territory strategy · Budget allocation · Release Runway project setup · DDEX/Atmos readiness. These are assigned internally during onboarding.
