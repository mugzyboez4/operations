Bio

From Artists · Bio field

Lancey Foux is an East London-raised recording artist and cultural trailblazer, known for his genre-defying sound that blends hip-hop, trap, and experimental influences. Once he picked up a mic, he didn't stop, kicking off his musical journey with early releases like Pink, First Day at Nursery, and Pink II.

In 2019, he broke into the global conversation with cult classic FRIEND OR FOUX, featuring standout tracks like "INDIA," "DREAM," and "GHETTO YOUT." His momentum continued with FIRST DEGREE, packed with bangers including "WHAT U WANT" feat. Skepta and "Don't TALK." With a loyal global fanbase, Lancey has solidified his place in both music and fashion.

As a trailblazer in UK rap, he's been instrumental in birthing a new, alternative sound that's redefining the genre. Lancey's viral hit "Lancey or Lancey" became a cultural phenomenon, showcasing his unique charisma and energy while resonating with fans worldwide. In 2024, his collaborative album The Conglomerate debuted at #1 on the UK rap album charts, featuring six tracks, including the project's highest-streaming songs. Additionally, his single "Black & Tan" with YT earned critical acclaim, landing spots on Pitchfork's 100 Best Songs of 2024 and NME's Best Songs of 2024. Lancey has collaborated with Kanye West, Teezo Touchdown, and Sexyy Red, further cementing his reputation as a dynamic and forward-thinking artist.

On the fashion front, Lancey has walked the runway and collaborated with iconic brands such as Givenchy, Diesel, Rains, MISBHV, and Rhude, among others. In the summer of 2025, Lancey performed at the iconic Reading & Leeds Festival, underscoring his presence on global stages. Known for pushing creative boundaries and leading the UK rap genre, Lancey continues to captivate audiences with his innovative artistry and fearless style, making him one of the most exciting talents of his generation.
