Black History Month February

RCA's roster has deep Black artist representation across hip-hop, R&B, soul, and pop. The choice for a BHM activation isn't who's eligible — it's which campaign angle. Editorial title highlights, catalog-focused playlist, capsule capsule pulling from multiple artists, or single-artist anchor moment all work.
SZATier-1 anchor
Strongest single-artist anchor option. Significant catalog, current relevance. CoR merch rights secured per Greenhouse deck. SZA has historically participated in Black-creator-platform campaigns; familiar territory for her team.
Chris Brown · Bryson Tiller · H.E.R.R&B trio
R&B catalog anchors. Could pair on themed compilation / playlist push. Chris Brown's catalog spans 15+ years; H.E.R. has Grammy-anchored prestige; Bryson Tiller's TrapSoul is a generational R&B benchmark.
Sleepy Hallow · Sleepy BrownHip-hop catalog
Hip-hop angle. Sleepy Hallow's Brooklyn drill catalog, Sleepy Brown's OutKast/Dungeon Family connection (legacy). Two different generations of Black hip-hop on the roster.
Khalid · Doja Cat · A$AP Rocky · Sleepy Brown · Becky G (Latin/Black)Cross-genre
Wider catalog spread for editorial / playlist play. A$AP Rocky's Don't Be Dumb cycle still active; Doja Cat brand recognition; Khalid's catalog. Becky G crosses BHM and Hispanic Heritage Month — worth flagging the dual relevance.
