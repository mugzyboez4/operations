04 · Cadence

How it runs

Operating model for the team while strategic time is split. Light cadence; more would create friction.

Sync
30 min, weekly
Standing slot. Build lead drives the agenda. Topics: shipped this week, blockers, next week's focus, escalations.
Friday note
Written status
Build lead writes a 5-bullet summary every Friday: shipped / blocked / next / escalations / questions for leadership.
Async
Slack & email
Slack for time-sensitive blockers. Email for stakeholder loops. Standing sync handles everything else.
