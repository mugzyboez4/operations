Age Distribution

The streaming audience is young but the IG audience is even younger. On Spotify, 18–22 (31.6%, index 158) and 13–17 (14.1%, index 262) dominate. On Instagram, 18–24 is 53% of followers and 13–17 over-indexes at 385. The social audience is a younger subset of the streaming audience — the content is reaching teens who haven’t fully converted to streaming yet.

Audience Psychographics

Derived from streaming demographics, geographic indexing, platform behavior, and fans-also-like clustering.

Who They Are

Gen Z women (18–24 core) in smaller US markets and Northern Europe. These are early-career music fans still building their taste identity — they follow artists as people first, listeners second. Discovery happens through IG Reels, TikTok sounds, and peer sharing, not editorial playlists. The rural/small-market US over-indexing suggests organic spread through friend networks rather than algorithmic metro penetration.

Values & Identity

Authenticity over polish. This audience follows Hailey because she feels real and relatable, not because of a hit song. The 84.8% female skew and 385 IG index for 13–17 year olds suggest identity-driven fandom — they see themselves in her. Brand partnerships should feel personal and unforced. Anything that reads as corporate or transactional will break the spell.

Platforms & Behavior

Instagram is the primary relationship layer — 23% engagement means the audience treats her posts like personal updates from a friend. TikTok is the discovery engine with 22.7% like-to-view. Both platforms dramatically outperform benchmarks. The audience is digitally native, attention-rich, and content-responsive. They will engage with anything she posts. The gap is converting that engagement into streaming behavior.

Cultural Context

Fans Also Like cluster (Bella Kay, STELLA LEFTY, Alyssa Grace) signals an emerging female pop micro-scene. These artists are discovering each other’s audiences simultaneously. The UK/Ireland corridor (Norway 299, Ireland 290, GB 193) aligns with a Northern European pop sensibility — clean production, emotional vocals, confessional songwriting. The predominantly White audience (78.3%, index 146) with minimal Hispanic representation (index 44) defines a specific cultural lane.
