03

How Things Flow

A few simple habits keep requests from getting lost and keep everyone moving in the same direction.

ONE FRONT DOOR

Start requests in one place

Got an ask? Send it through one intake channel rather than scattered texts. One front door means it's logged, routed, and never forgotten — and you can see where it stands.

LEAD TIME

Earlier beats perfect

The single biggest thing that helps a campaign: lead time. A rough heads-up today is worth more than a polished ask the night before.

TURNAROUND

What to expect back

Most requests get an acknowledgment quickly, with a real answer or timeline to follow. If something's urgent, flag it — we'll fast-track and tell you what's realistic.

APPROVALS

The big calls stay yours

Decisions about your music and image stay with you and your team, alongside the label leads. Campaign Ops never approves or overrides — we just make sure the right people see the right thing at the right time.

04

Planning a Release

Every campaign has a shape. Knowing the phases — and the lead time each needs — makes the whole thing calmer.

Strategy
~12–16 weeks out
  • Release date locked
  • Rollout strategy set
  • Creative direction underway
  • Press approach mapped
Build
~8–10 weeks out
  • Campaign plan live
  • DSP & playlist pitching
  • Artwork final (~8 wks)
  • Pre-save / pre-order up
Deliver
~10 days out
  • Final masters delivered
  • Metadata & credits locked
  • Platform ingestion
  • Final checks
Launch & Sustain
Release week & beyond
  • Drop-day coordination
  • Press & social rollout
  • Momentum moments
  • Debrief & next beat

What We Need From You

Getting these in on time is the quietest superpower in a campaign.

Audio

  • Final masters — stereo & spatial
  • Instrumentals & clean versions
  • Stems if available

Artwork

  • Cover art (high-res)
  • Alternate / square crops
  • Logo & title treatments

Video & Motion

  • Music video / visualizer
  • Vertical social cuts
  • Behind-the-scenes footage

Promo & Social

  • Artist-captured content
  • Announcement assets
  • Press / bio photos

Metadata & Credits

  • Songwriter / producer splits
  • Features & credits confirmed
  • Track titles & sequencing

Delivery
